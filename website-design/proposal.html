My Personal Site: Project Proposal

Last Updated: 06/06/2022


What Is It?

For my WebTech term project, I would like to create a personal site where I can post my resume and projects, and also maintain a blog.

Why This?

I have always seen personal websites as a very cool thing. It can be a terrific canvas to express yourself, even more than social media.

Who Is My Targert Market?

I would like my friends and family to visit the site, especially those who I do not keep in touch with at a regular basis. I would also like employers or coworkers to see the page.

What Content Do I Plan to Include?

  • Intro Page (Short Description of Myself)
  • Resume Page (Pretty Much What I'd Put on LinkedIn
  • Projects Page (Includes the projects I'm currently working on)
  • Blog Page (Small blog I hope to maintain)
  • Gallery (Photos of places I've been)
  • Restaurant Recommendations (Good Restaurants I've Been To)
  • Possible: list of services I want to self-host for public use
